Hi,

it seams that you are trying to plot x,y,z as locations, but do not have 
any further data. So dx does not know what to plot at that locations. 
Two work-arounds: invent a third column to hold a constant data value, 
or, better for this case, use x,y as locations and z as data value 
(using mark or by modifying the importer). Probably this is what you 
plot means anyways, height as a function of x,y?
